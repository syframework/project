Privacy policy

Cookies

A cookie is a small computer file which allows to analyze the behavior of users during the visit a web site, or the use of a software or a mobile application.

There are 3 categories of cookie on our site :

  • Cookies used for the proper functioning of the site, to manage the connection to the site by example
  • Cookies statistics tools that allow you to have the number of visitors to the site
  • Cookies of advertising tools that allow you to show ads more relevant

You can block cookies by configuring your browser, for this, go first of all on the page Cookies & you, it is a tool that will detect the version of your browser and to provide you with the approach to be followed in function of it. Depending on your choice to either disable all cookies or only third party cookies, you no longer have to follow the steps that are displayed.
